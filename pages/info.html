I think art is anything that activates our senses through aesthetic values.

My name is Mahsa Mirkhaninejad, aGraphic Designer based in California. Creativity has always been a huge part of my life, which has prompted my passion for Graphic Design. As a teenager, I was always the first to help others with unique and innovative solutions to their problems. In order to relate to others and aid them in making better decisions, I would be considerate of their circumstances.

I realized that people can be impressed by the creation of an exceptional product, but an engaging advertisement is what sells the product at hand, as it sparks interest across a wider audience. Visual communication is one of the most effective ways to tell stories and relay information.

I personally believe that life is full of great moments, which is reflected through the consistent use of playful colors, typography, and space within my pieces. In my leisure time, I'm usually crafting fine-jewelry with precious stones or working with graphic design software such as Visual Studio Code, Adobe Photoshop, and Illustrator. I am determined to service others with my expertise and inspire hope in the world of design.
